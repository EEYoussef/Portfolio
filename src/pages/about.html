A little bit about myself.

Hello there I am Eman Youssef and I am very passionate about seeing my imaginations come to life. I'm constantly learning new things every day and using them to bring happiness to those around me. I love connecting with like minded people. I am motivated and unafraid of sharing my ideas with the world. I'm a self-taught designer and have learned web development ,automated testing , Python and Graphical design by attending workshops, reading blogs, and applying these to projects in my spare time. I am a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to development.

Programming for me is both science and art, it requires analytical thinking of a scientist and conceptual thinking as an artist. In my opinion being an artist is essential to come up with big ideas and approach them in a unique and flexible ways. And to apply certain methodologies as needed and consider testing and debugging from a logical perspective is also essential.
